About Our Website
Welcome to our website! We understand that navigating the original BMCC website to join clubs and connect with mentors may be a troublesome task as information is not easily available. That's why we have embarked on a mission to make this process more accessible and user-friendly. Our goal is to enhance the functionality of the clubs website by providing a platform that allows students to effortlessly join existing clubs or create new ones. Our simplified login system allows students to easily explore and discover a wide range of clubs and mentors that align with their interests. We are confident that our website will not only streamline the process but also foster meaningful connections between students and mentors.
